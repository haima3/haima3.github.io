MoeBee Network

MoeBee Network(aka Haima Global Network, AS139328) is operated by MoeBee Network, a tiny geek team.

Peering

We have an open peering policy. You can peer with us through Internet eXchanges (listed in IX section) that we have in common.

For your convenience, here are more info about peering.

  • ASN: 139328
  • AS-SET: AS-MOEBEE-CN
  • Suggested IPv4 Prefix limit: 1000
  • Suggested IPv6 Prefix limit: 1000

Service

LIR service (ASN and IPv6 block) is also provided, please contact us by support mailbox for more information.

IP Transit service (IPv6 only, and only available in some regions) is also provided, please contact us by support mailbox for more information.
